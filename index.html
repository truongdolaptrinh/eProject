Life is like
climbing a mountain

“The higher you climb on the mountain, the harder the wind blows . Hard to climb, but once you get to the top, the view is beautiful. Everyone wants to live on top of the mountain, but all the happiness and growth occurs while you’re climbing it.”

Extraordinary
things

“Mountains are only a problem when they are bigger than you. You should develop yourself so much that you become bigger than the mountains you face. When you stand at the bottom of the mountain and look up at the mountaintop, the path looks hard and stony, and the top is obscured by clouds. But when you reach the top and you look down, you realize that there are a thousand paths that could have brought you to that place.”

The best view
comes after
the hardest climb

Everybody wants to reach the peak, but there is no growth on the top of a mountain. It is in the valley that we slog through the lush grass and rich soil, learning and becoming what enables us to summit life’s next peak.”

Be Yourself
Do What You Want

“If you are faced with a mountain, you have many options. You can climb on it and cross to the other shore. You can go around it. You can dig underneath it. You can fly over it. You can blow it up. You can ignore it and pretend it's not there. You can turn around and go back the way you came. Or you can stay in the mountain and make it your home.”
